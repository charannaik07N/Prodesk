## Design Context

### Users

- Primary users are job seekers (students, freshers, and early-career professionals) preparing applications quickly.
- They use this tool during active job search sessions, often under time pressure.
- Their core job-to-be-done is to convert resume + role details into a clear, tailored cover letter with minimal effort.

### Brand Personality

- Professional, clear, and supportive.
- Emotional goal: reduce stress and increase confidence before submitting applications.
- Personality keywords: trustworthy, calm, practical.

### Aesthetic Direction

- Light, editorial, and clean interface with warm neutral surfaces.
- Minimal color accents for action/focus only; no decorative gradients.
- High readability and tidy spacing over visual effects.

### Design Principles

- Clarity first: every element should earn its place and support task completion.
- Calm hierarchy: strong typographic structure, restrained accents, minimal noise.
- Consistent interactions: all controls have visible focus, hover, active, loading, and disabled behavior.
- Accessible defaults: readable sizes, clear contrast, and keyboard-friendly forms.
- Practical polish: optimize for fast completion over visual novelty.

### Quality Bar

- MVP-plus polish: clean, reliable, and launch-ready without over-engineering.
