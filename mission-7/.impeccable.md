## Design Context

### Users

Employees creating an account inside an internal or company-facing workflow. The form should help them complete registration quickly and without friction.

### Brand Personality

Calm, efficient, trustworthy.

### Aesthetic Direction

Light editorial minimalism with a clean, professional feel. Use a restrained neutral palette, one strong dark accent, strong spacing, and clear hierarchy.

### Design Principles

- Keep the form easy to scan and complete.
- Prefer clarity and contrast over decoration.
- Use a single, intentional accent color for hierarchy.
- Make validation feedback obvious and polite.
- Preserve a clean, responsive layout on smaller screens.
